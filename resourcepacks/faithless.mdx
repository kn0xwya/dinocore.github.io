This pack aims to make Minecraft accessible to everyone, including Full Colorblindness, Arachnophobia, Trypophobia, Hard of hearing and Dyslexia. This pack gives nearly everything a unique texture for variance in builds and easy differentiation, which may bring a fresh RPG experience, character and personality to everything in your game. Heavy inspiration from Programmer Art and music videos such as Fallen Kingdom.
