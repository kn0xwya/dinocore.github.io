## Useful Guides

[To fix black eyes, follow this guide](https://github.com/IthanMendoza/Fresh-Moves/wiki/Fix-Black-Eyes).
For a video demonstration go to this page in the wiki of the resourcepack.

[Fix Black Eyes · IthanMendoza/Fresh-Moves Wiki (github.com)](https://github.com/IthanMendoza/Fresh-Moves/wiki/Fix-Black-Eyes)

If you want your hand to stop moving in the first person perspective. [Go to this page in the wiki to disable it.](https://github.com/IthanMendoza/Fresh-Moves/wiki/Fix-Moving-1st-Person-Hand)

[Fix Moving 1st Person Hand · IthanMendoza/Fresh-Moves Wiki (github.com)](https://github.com/IthanMendoza/Fresh-Moves/wiki/Fix-Moving-1st-Person-Hand)
